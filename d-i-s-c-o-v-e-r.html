LIFE PARENTS
As an only child I really consider my parents as my life because they are my strength who always support me at every stage of my life. And I cannot imagine my life without them. I also can’t wait to bring back all the sacrifices that they have made for me.
PET MJ When Mj comes into our life it's like a blessing for our family. As he brings joy and unconditional love for us. He belongs to the breed of shih tzu. Mj is such a sweet puppy that loves to travel and eat especially ice cream

HOBBY BAKING Since I was a child I have been a little bit curious about baking because of my grandmother who also loves baking. So when I am given a chance to learn baking in high school I actually grab the opportunity. Until I tried to test in NC II and I am proudly to say that I’ve became a NCII passer in bread and pastry.
FAVORITE PASTA Spaghetti has been one of my favorite dishes since childhood and since it is effortless to create, delicious and nutritious meal the whole family can savor. Aside from that, me and my mom really love to cook this dish and try new recipes. Fun fact: I can say that I can eat this dish all day.
INTEREST CAR I really didn’t expect that I would be interested in cars. Some may think a lady like me is not as usual interested in this kind of thing. But since my dad taught me to drive it makes me love it even more and it’s cool to have a knowledge about cars .And it’s really cute because me and my dad have this kind of bond such as going to car shows. For me a car is not just a utility, its a ticket to adventure and creating new memories.
PLAN TRAVEL One of my dreams is to travel around the world with my family because I believe traveling is one way to enjoy and discover different things and also a bonding of a family. Yes it is amazing because you’ll get a chance to visit new places, eat different kinds of foods and experience different cultures. Also me and my parents loved to travel so I can’t wait to sees different places with my lovely parents.
